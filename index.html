Visit Nanjing

Nanjing is a sophisticated metropolis where the remains of past empires and ancient traditions gracefully intersect with contemporary Chinese culture.

Top places to visit in Nanjing

Jiming Temple

One of the oldes buddist temples in China. I love visiting this temple when I was only a child without knowing its deep-down meaning.

Nanjing Museum

One of the three biggest museums in China. It is the place to see and feel the richness of the Chinese cultures.

Nanjing City Wall

Taking a stroll on the city wall surely takes you back to history.
